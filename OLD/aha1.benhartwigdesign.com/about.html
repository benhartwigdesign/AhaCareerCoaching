About Claire

Claire Peters

Master's in Counseling, University of Wisconsin-Madison
Bachelor's in Journalism, University of Wisconsin-Madison
Certification by the Career Coach Institute

I have been there!

I didn’t know what I wanted to be when I grew up until I was way past grown up. College for me was filled with confusion about majors and careers. And I never sought help.

Eventually, I picked up a book called Wishcraft by Barbara Sher. As I read about others who had struggled to find their place in the world, it dawned on me that I could help others with the same concerns. I had found my calling!

I enrolled in graduate school at the University of Wisconsin-Madison and earned a master’s degree in counseling, with an emphasis in higher education. I also received specialized training from the Career Coach Institute.

After graduate school, I became a career counselor at my alma mater, UW-Madison, honing my skills and finding immense satisfaction helping literally thousands of students cut through indecision and find direction.

I am passionate about helping college students, and I would love to help you!
