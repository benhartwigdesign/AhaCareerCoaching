About Claire

I have worked with thousands of college students and adults to help them clarify their career dreams. With a master’s degree in counseling from the UW-Madison, I have an emphasis in higher education and career counseling. I have led dozens of workshops on major and career exploration and decision-making. By using a combination of empathy, powerful questions, and gentle confrontation I help students and clients explore their beliefs, attitudes, passions, and purpose, and motivate them to action.
