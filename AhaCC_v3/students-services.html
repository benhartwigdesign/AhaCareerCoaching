How I Can Help Students

What Aha! Career Coaching
Can Do For You

Wouldn't it be great to feel less stressed and more in control of your major and career decisions? You can!

When we work together, I will use an inspiring combination of empathy, powerful questions, and gentle confrontation to help you:

  • Strategically explore majors and programs related to your interests and skills

  • Clarify goals and build confidence in choosing a direction

  • Identify what's important to you, and how your values relate to majors and careers

  • Learn about career paths you may not have considered

  • Gain confidence in your unique strengths

  • Identify ways to get important experiences outside the classroom

  • Clarify and resolve obstacles that keep you stuck

  • Overcome barriers that keep you from getting the most out of college
